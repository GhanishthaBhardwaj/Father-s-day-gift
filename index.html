03 — The Letter

Last June.

Last June, I failed, and for a while, I lost my way. I questioned myself, my dreams, and everything I thought I was capable of.

There were voices that filled me with doubt and tried to convince me that I was not enough.

But you never listened to those voices.

You trusted me when I couldn’t trust myself. You saw potential in me that I could no longer see, and when I was standing at a crossroads, you gently showed me the right path.

You never saw me as my failure. You saw me as the person I could still become.

Even today, I don’t think I believe in myself as much as you believe in me.

And that has been one of the greatest gifts of my life.

Thank you for standing beside me, for never doubting me, and for holding on to my dreams when I was ready to let go of them.

I hope one day I become everything you already believe I can be.

Happy Father’s Day, Papa.
